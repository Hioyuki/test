Site Name: Tsunoshima Memory

This name captures the emotional and scenic beauty of Tsunoshima, a picturesque island in Shimonoseki. The word “Memory” emphasizes the site's goal: to preserve visitor experiences and local charm.

Optional Domain: tsunoshimamemory.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as a digital archive and travel hub for Tsunoshima. It provides information on scenic spots, local cafes, and visitor recommendations, and also encourages users to share their memories and photos. It helps connect tourists and locals while promoting Tsunoshima’s charm year-round.

Scenarios

  • “I’m planning a weekend trip to Yamaguchi. What can I do and see on Tsunoshima Island?”
    This visitor needs access to highlights, directions, and local tips.
  • “I visited Tsunoshima five years ago and it was beautiful. Are there any photo galleries or stories from other visitors I can browse or contribute to?”
    This returning visitor is emotionally engaged and looking to relive or contribute to the memory archive.

Color Schema

  • #006699 (Ocean Blue): Used for headers and primary branding elements to reflect the color of the sea and sky.
  • #f0f8ff (Alice Blue): Used as the background color to create a light, calm feel reminiscent of a breezy island day.

Typography

  • Georgia: Used for main headings to provide an elegant, classic look.
  • Segoe UI: Used for body text and subheadings, offering modern readability and web-optimized design.
